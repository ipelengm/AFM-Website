About Us

Formerly known then as the house of prayer, afm upper room church was founded in may 2008 with just a number of 12 people consisting of mostly student pastors at the auckland park theological seminary, johannesburg. Upper room was a result of the english service which was established by pastor ntokozo and other pastors after a gap was discovered that there was only an afrikaans service at the church. Today, the church has climbed from 12 congregants to reaching the numbers, achievements and developments it has obtained over the years due to long hours of heated and spirit filled prayer sessions. Afm upper room is now led by pastor nkanyiso ngcobo who was formally ordained in 2012 and has since been the leader of the church to date.

Vision

To reach and influence our city through Jesus Christ,by building a large church that is Christ centered, changing minds and empowering future leaders who are God fearing.

Mission.

To bring to Jesus, making them participants in the family of Jesus Christ,developing them to Christ like maturity, and prepare/equip them for the work of ministry in the church and their sphere of influence in the world,in order to magnify God's name.

Vision Scripture.

He said to them "Go into the world and preach the gospel to all creation" Mark 16:15.

The Leadership

A selfless and growing church attaining balance by bibical based doctrine,loving God,loving people and loving life,all for the glory of God.
